My Projects

Food Delivery Chatbot

In this project, I designed and implemented a sophisticated Food Delivery Chatbot that leverages Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Dialogflow to deliver an engaging and user-friendly ordering experience. The chatbot is architected using FastAPI, which facilitates efficient API connections and ensures seamless communication between the user interface and backend services.

This solution is crafted to understand and respond to user inquiries in natural language, enabling customers to place orders effortlessly. The chatbot enhances the overall user experience by providing real-time assistance and guiding users through the ordering process. Additionally, its scalable design allows for easy integration with various food delivery platforms, making it a versatile tool for businesses looking to improve customer engagement and streamline operations.

Mini Chatbot

In this project, I developed a Mini Chatbot utilizing a Large Language Model (LLM) from Groq to deliver intelligent and responsive interactions. The user-friendly interface is designed using Streamlit, providing a seamless web experience for users to engage with the chatbot.

This innovative solution harnesses the capabilities of advanced natural language understanding, allowing it to comprehend and respond to user queries effectively. By providing quick and accurate answers, the Mini Chatbot enhances user engagement and satisfaction. The flexible design also enables easy deployment across various platforms, making it a valuable asset for businesses seeking to improve customer interactions and support.
